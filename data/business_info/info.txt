LOYALTY PROGRAM:

How It Works:
Earn 1 point for every dollar spent. Points never expire. Use points for free drinks and food.

Bronze Tier (Free to Join):
- Earn: 1x points (1 point per dollar)
- Benefits: Birthday free drink, early access to app features
- Rewards: 50 points = free drink, 25 points = free pastry

Silver Tier (Unlock at $200 total spend):
- Earn: 1x points
- Benefits: 5% off all iced beverages, birthday free drink plus free pastry, surprise seasonal treats
- Rewards: Same redemption as Bronze
- Special: Monthly double-points days

Gold Tier (Unlock at $500 total spend):
- Earn: 1.5x points (earn 50% faster)
- Benefits: 10% off all hot beverages, free pastry coupon monthly, priority customer service, early access to new menu items
- Rewards: 40 points = free drink, 20 points = free pastry
- Special: Quarterly exclusive tasting events

Platinum Tier (Unlock at $1000 total spend):
- Earn: 2x points (double speed)
- Benefits: 15% off ALL drinks, one free drink per month automatically, free birthday cake, VIP event invitations, dedicated support line
- Rewards: 30 points = free drink, 15 points = free pastry
- Special: Annual members-only party, bring a guest free drink monthly
